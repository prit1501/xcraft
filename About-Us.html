Our Story

Our Co-Founder and CEO, JD Claridge, has always been driven by his passion for flight and innovation. At a young age, he built his own hang gliders with bedsheets and scrap tubing. In 2005, he built a fixed-wing drone out of hobby parts for filming real estate in his town, which was an unconventional method at that time.
